Detailed Description

Selection of muon neutrinos with 0 pion and at least one proton in the final state, largely based on CC0piNpSelection Author: Sebastien Prince (sprin.nosp@m.ce@f.nosp@m.as.ha.nosp@m.rvar.nosp@m.d.edu)
